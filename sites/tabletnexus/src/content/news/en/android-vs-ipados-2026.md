Choosing between an **Android** tablet and an **iPad** is no longer decided on the spec sheet: the two worlds have grown much closer. What matters more is understanding how you'll use the device.

## Apps and productivity

iPadOS keeps an edge on tablet-optimized professional apps. Android has closed the gap on windowed multitasking, but app quality varies far more.

## Ecosystem

If you already own an iPhone and a Mac, Apple's integration is hard to beat. In the Android world, continuity depends on the maker: some suites are excellent, others just adequate.

## Price

This is where Android stays unbeatable in the mid-range: you'll find great tablets at prices iPadOS never reaches. At the very top, prices even out.

## In short

Want the least hassle and already own other Apple devices? iPad. Want flexibility and to spend less? Android — but choose the maker carefully.

Spending little doesn't mean buying badly, as long as you know what you're giving up. Here's how to navigate the budget tier in 2026.

## What to look for under $300

- **Display:** at least Full HD, bright enough for indoor use.
- **RAM:** 6 GB is the minimum for smooth multitasking over time.
- **Updates:** check how many years of software support the maker promises.

## Where corners get cut

In this tier you almost always give up premium build quality, better speakers and fast charging. Those are acceptable trade-offs for couch use.

## The advice

A last-year mid-range model on sale beats a new entry-level one: for the same money, the display and performance make a difference every day.
